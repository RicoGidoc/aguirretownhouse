🏡 Versailles Alabang – Project Details

📍 Location

Versailles Alabang Village is located along Daang Hari Road near Las Piñas, Alabang, and Cavite border. Across Ayala Alabang Village. Accessible via MCX, SLEX, and Skyway.

👉 Travel time: Makati/BGC 20–30 mins | NAIA ~20 mins

🌟 Project Overview

A 30-hectare gated luxury subdivision with Mediterranean-inspired design and flood-free elevated location.

👉 For luxury living, families, and investors.

🏊 Amenities

Clubhouse, swimming pools, gym, spa, tennis court, basketball court, parks, playgrounds, cabanas.

📌 Nearby Places

🏫 Schools: De La Salle Zobel, San Beda Alabang
🏥 Hospitals: Asian Hospital, Alabang Medical Center
🛍️ Malls: ATC, Festival Mall, SM Southmall
💼 Business: Filinvest City, Northgate Cyberzone

🚗 Accessibility

Near MCX exit, direct Daang Hari access, close to SLEX & Skyway. Easy travel to Makati, BGC, and airport.
